Profile

About

Haohong Lai is a PhD candidate in Translation and Intercultural Studies at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona. His doctoral project develops an empirically grounded digital literacy framework for translation and language teaching based on expert interviews.

His research brings together translation technologies, machine translation quality evaluation, post-editing, prompt-based translation practices, and the wider relationship between philosophy, technology, and translation studies.

Research Projects

Collaborator, 2025-2029

GENERATESE-2LT: Description of Generatese in Second Languages and Translation

The project investigates linguistic and extralinguistic features of GenAI-generated texts and develops a methodological framework for text quality assessment in multilingual contexts, with a focus on translation between English, Spanish, and Catalan, and on Chinese as a second language for Spanish-speaking learners.
